# assets/ — Required Files

Place the following files in this folder for the portfolio to display correctly:

## ids-project.jpg  ← IDS PROJECT SCREENSHOT
- Screenshot of your IDS deep learning project (dashboard, results, model output, etc.)
- Rename to exactly: `ids-project.jpg`

## soc-project.jpg  ← SOC PROJECT SCREENSHOT
- Screenshot of your SOC dashboard, Kibana/Grafana panel, or architecture diagram
- Rename to exactly: `soc-project.jpg`

---

## profile.jpg  ← YOUR PHOTO
- A square or portrait photo of yourself
- Recommended: 400x400px minimum, JPG or PNG
- It will display at ~300px wide in the About section
- Rename to exactly: `profile.jpg`

## favicon.png  ← BROWSER TAB ICON
- A small square icon (32x32 or 64x64px)
- You can use a shield icon or your initials
- Rename to exactly: `favicon.png`

## Nguyen_Thanh_Danh_CV.pdf  ← YOUR RESUME (optional)
- Export your CV to PDF and place it here
- The "View Resume" button in the About section links to this file
- Rename to exactly: `Nguyen_Thanh_Danh_CV.pdf`

---
All other styling and content is already in index.html, styles.css, and main.js.
No build step required — just open index.html in your browser!
